---
type: Minor breaking change
---

The `ready` and `error` events can now be emitted multiple times during a component's lifetime when the watchdog is active.

By default, the editor is wrapped with a watchdog that automatically restarts it after a crash. As a result, both events may fire repeatedly — `error` after each crash, and `ready` after each successful watchdog restart — rather than only once during the component's mount/unmount lifecycle. If your handlers for these events assume they will be called at most once, they may need to be updated to handle repeated invocations.
